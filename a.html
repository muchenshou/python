话题 : I’m So Broke! 我要“破产了”！（一）

2013-06-02

K: Hey Sam do you want to go with me to see a movie this weekend?

S: Ah, I have been dying to go see a movie. But I have been sup er broke recently. I think I am going to spend this weekend looking for a job.

K: A job! Are you ready for that kind of responsibility?

S: I know finding a job will mean more responsibility, but I think it is a necessary step that I need to take. I can’t rely on my parents for everything.

K: Yeah that’s true. I guess I wouldn’t mind the satisfaction of earning my own money.

S: My pare ntsare ok with me finding a job. They think it will be a good experience for me. And they said they don’t care how I use the money.

K: Wow! Your parents are pretty cool. I am surprised they don’t want you to put some of the money you make towards a college fund.

S: Well, I think they want to see if I will use my money wisely or not.

K: So will you?
